About Me

My interest in Computer Science can be traced back to 2014 when The Imitation Game was released. I was amazed by the story, and from there, I got obsessed with the idea that one could model complex questions under mathematical frameworks so neatly and logically. Not until high school did I first got in touch with real programming. My first hand-on project is writing a web-scrapping script for past-paper downloading in Python due to the need for massive practice preparing for the IGCSE exams. I could still remember the excitement in realizing that I could solve real-world problem through coding, which prompts me to learn more about Computer Science.


In my spare time, I like to play video games and am currently playing Lost Judgement. My interest in games leads me to self-study Unity at the start of 2024. Aside from games, I love playing badminton, a sport I started playing since primary school.
